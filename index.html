Our Mission

STEM for
Everyone.

We aim to bring STEM opportunities to underrepresented communities and empower young children with a vision for their future in science, technology, engineering, and math.

Our Mission

Why We
Exist.

GearForward was built on a simple belief: access to STEM education should never depend on where you're from or what you can afford.

Mission Statement

"At the core of our organization lies a mission to ensure that STEM education is accessible, inclusive, and empowering for all individuals — particularly those who have historically been marginalized or overlooked in the fields of science, technology, engineering, and mathematics."

Our main objective is to engage students and communities that have had minimal to no exposure to STEM. Whether this is due to financial constraints or insufficient access to STEM education, we strive to aid those in these situations by developing programs and environments where every student feels welcomed, motivated, and capable of achieving success in STEM.

We are actively pursuing the expansion of our reach by establishing new chapters throughout Oregon and beyond. By creating a network of student-led teams grounded in local communities, we can gain a better understanding of the specific needs of each area and customize our initiatives to maximize our impact.

In addition to our outreach and educational efforts, we are dedicated to offering free resources — such as STEM kits, mentorship, and interactive workshops — to students and schools that require assistance. We firmly believe that financial constraints should never hinder the learning process. This is why we provide all our programs and materials at no cost to participants, made possible through the generosity of donors, volunteers, and partners who align with our vision.

Ultimately, our aim extends beyond merely teaching; we seek to unlock opportunities. We aspire to help young individuals by connecting them with opportunities and equipping them with the tools and education necessary to pursue their aspirations. By investing in underserved communities today, we hope to inspire the diverse leaders, thinkers, and innovators of the future.

Core Values

What Guides
Everything We Do

01

Accessibility

Every program, resource, and opportunity we offer is free. Financial constraints will never be a barrier to learning with GearForward.

02

Inclusion

We build spaces where students from all backgrounds feel welcomed, capable, and seen — especially those historically underrepresented in STEM.

03

Empowerment

We don't just teach skills — we help students discover what they're capable of and equip them with tools to pursue their own futures.

04

Community

Student-led chapters mean we're grounded in the communities we serve. Local insight drives every decision we make.

05

Innovation

Hands-on building and real competition push students to think creatively, fail fast, and keep improving — skills that last a lifetime.

06

Impact

We measure success by the opportunities we unlock and the futures we help shape — not just the number of students we reach.
